As an open source, PROTOMAK received some sponsorship from the community. Here follows a list of sponsors who contributed to the success of PROTOMAK. Thank you all.

Atlassian - JIRA and Greenhopper

PROTOMAK wants to warmly thank Atlassian for providing a free, unlimited, open source license for JIRA and Greenhopper. This shows the value that PROTOMAK brings to the open source community and the power of open-source software.

Sonatype OSS

Nexus OSS provides Open Source Software with the infrastructure required to deploy SNAPSHOTS and releases to Maven Central. The full documentation on how to use Nexus OSS is available here.

PODAM

PODAM is the de-facto Java testing framework to fill POJOs and JavaBeans with data.
